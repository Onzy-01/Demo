Our Story

Habidade is a neighbourhood café located in Sector 104, Noida, thoughtfully designed as a peaceful urban habitat. Whether you're catching up with friends, working remotely, or simply enjoying your favorite coffee, every corner is crafted to make you feel comfortable.

Enjoy lush greenery, natural sunlight, cozy wooden seating, quiet workspaces, authentic cuisine, handcrafted coffee, kombuchas, grilled specialties, and warm hospitality that keeps guests coming back.
